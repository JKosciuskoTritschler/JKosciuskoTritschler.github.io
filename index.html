Gordon Gray Career Consulting

Career Counseling for clarification/focus and job search coaching

Career Clarification and Focus

  • Assess each client's past career(s) and clarify where and how to find greater satisfaction through their values.
  • Help people identify their career skills – those they enjoy as well as those in which they excel.
  • Help clients’ introspection and reflection through personal interpretation of established assessments:
    • The Strong Interest Indicator
    • The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator
  • Gain confidence, clarity and focus in the clients’ career management.

--Socrates: “Know Thyself”

Job Search Assistance

For those who have clear career goals and are in an active job search, I can assist with:

  • Resume writing, editing, revisions, and tailoring
  • Employer Correspondence and cover letters
  • Interviewing preparation, skills, and practice
  • Effective use of the internet in the job search
  • Networking skills and opportunities
  • Gordon offers a deep Boulder network and years of local job market experience
  • Gordon offers coaching and resources on job seeking tactics, methods, and strategies that have proven successful for others.

I can offer significant resources and assistance on job seeking methods, strategies and tactics that have proven helpful for others, overcoming what is usually a long and lonesome process.

Among Gordon's Clients:

  • Individuals considering a career change seeking satisfaction, relationships, and social relevancy.
  • Individuals pursuing new careers due to layoff, relocation, or other life changing events.
  • Mid-career professionals considering a career change.
  • People new to Boulder who looking for deep knowledge of the local job markets, industries, and networks.
  • Professionals making the transition to an encore career.
  • Those leaving the workforce and entering a stimulating and enjoyable retirement.
  • Young Adults entering the workforce or working toward a career
  • Collegiate students tailoring their experiences for a more competitive edge in their post-college careers.

Gordon Gray

Gordon has over 30 years of career development experience. He was Director of Career Services at the University of Colorado at Boulder for 22 years. Previously he held the same position at George Washington University and The Catholic University of America both in Washington, DC.

After retirement from CU-Boulder, Gordon served as the Program Director for Career Services at the YWCA of Boulder County for eleven years. There he assisted over 2000 clients with their career planning and job seeking.

Gordon was honored by Boulder Business and Professional Women in 2010 as “Man of The Year” for his contribution to the career needs in Boulder. Gordon also received the “Rich Feller Award” in 2011 from his peers in the Colorado Career Development Association for his commitment to excellence in career development.
